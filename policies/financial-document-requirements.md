# Financial Document Requirements

## 2.1 Current financial statement requirement

The package must include a financial statement that identifies the company,
annual revenue in EUR, and the reporting year. Employee count should be captured
when the statement provides it.

## 2.2 Revenue comparison

Revenue is compared across submitted documents after currency and numeric
normalisation. Values whose symmetric percentage difference is no more than two
percent may be treated as consistent; values outside that tolerance require
review.

## 2.3 Reporting-period evidence

The reporting year must be supported by text in the financial statement. An
absent reporting year is incomplete evidence and must not be inferred from the
upload date or another document.

## 2.4 Financial-field corrections

A reviewer correction must preserve the originally extracted value and its
evidence. The effective corrected value and the review decision are recorded
separately for auditability.
