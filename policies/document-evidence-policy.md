# Document Evidence Policy

## 6.1 Accepted V1 documents

V1 accepts synthetic, digitally generated PDFs with extractable text. Scanned or
image-only documents require OCR and are outside the supported V1 workflow.

## 6.2 Evidence citations

User-facing citations use one-based page numbers. A citation contains the source
document identifier and a concise source span that a reviewer can locate on the
specified page.

## 6.3 Source preservation

Uploaded source files and every duplicate are preserved. Processing produces a
separate page-text representation and must not mutate the source document.

## 6.4 Policy use

Retrieved policy text explains the relevance of verified findings. Policy
retrieval does not determine whether documents are missing, compare submitted
values, or create new verified findings.
