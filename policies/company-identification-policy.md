# Company Identification Policy

## 3.1 Legal identity evidence

The company extract is the required source for legal company name, registration
number, and incorporation date. The application form must also state the company
name and registration number.

## 3.2 Registration-number consistency

Registration numbers are compared after case and presentation separators are
normalised. Leading zeroes remain significant. Different effective registration
numbers are a high-priority identity conflict requiring human review.

## 3.3 Company-name consistency

Company names may be compared after Unicode, case, whitespace, punctuation,
ampersand, and legal-form presentation normalisation. Normalisation supports
comparison but does not erase the original submitted names or evidence.

## 3.4 Incorporation-date evidence

The incorporation date must be represented as an ISO date and supported by an
extract citation. If the required value is absent, the extract is incomplete.
